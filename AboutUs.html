The Ratnamala Educational and Cultural Society is serving the people through education from 2002 onwards. it has taken another step by starting NALANDA ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL. The School has been established with Library, science lab, Computer Lab, Sports room hostel and travelling convenience to the expected level. We are proud that our institution with well Qualiﬁed and committed faculty and hi-tech infrastructure enable our students to achieve and further their career in the challenging world. With this note of confidence, we warmly welcome our fresher’s and sure we are that, they will enjoy the high quality of education imported in the various disciplines and upon that foundation, build a great future for themselves.

Here, we teach the students The FOUR PILLARS of Success. Hard Work, Persistence, Commitment to a Vision, Belief. The school has been established with all the amenities like Library, science lab, Computer Lab, Sports room, hostel and travelling convenience to the expected level.

To bring the best of education to the rural people with the best equipment and teaching to bring out, the best out of the student and to make the student aware that he/she is no less equipped to deal with the world than his/her counter-part in a Metropolis. That is the guiding spirit of this institute . “Luceat Lux Vestra” the motto of the school (let your light shine) clearly establishes that we want the students to aim high and be achievers in their respective fields.

MISSION

The core purpose of education is to provide children with a learning environment which brings out the best in them. We strongly believe that a curriculum of excellence is central to the education of children to face the challenges of ensuing century with confidence and strength of character.

VISION

Our vision is to provide children with an education that fosters academic excellence, ensures their well-being and all-round development.
